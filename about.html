About Us

Introduction

GUIDING ME Co.,Ltd is an education consultancy started its operation in 13 November 2022 in Mandalay, Myanmar. Our Company set up with young entrepreneurs who have foreign experiences in overseas education. Thought we are young and fresh Company we have huge confidence for our Myanmar students in providing face to face pre-course counselling supporting and virtual coaching for choosing best academic decision to our students who would like to study Abroad. We believe that education is the key factor to change one's future. So we create a platform for those who desire to seek excellent quality in tuition, student services, qualifications and career prospects post qualifications.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to place right student at right education provider. We will create a customer-first environment where the staff is energetic, caring and has a student-focused attitude while celebrating diversity, embracing technology and promoting professional best practices.
